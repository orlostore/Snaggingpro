Terms of Engagement

Dear Valued Client, please review the following terms before the inspection proceeds.

Site Readiness

The hirer shall ensure that water and electricity connections are fully operational in the unit on the date of inspection. These are essential for completing all MEP and functional checks.

If the team arrives on-site and power or water is unavailable, the inspection will proceed on all accessible and possible items. No payment deduction shall be made for items that could not be tested due to utility unavailability.

Payment Terms

The full inspection fee is due in advance prior to the inspection date. No inspection will be conducted without confirmed full payment. Payment confirms acceptance of these terms.

Access Obligations

The hirer is responsible for providing full and unobstructed access to all areas of the unit on the agreed inspection date and time. This includes but is not limited to: all rooms, balconies, roof terrace, utility rooms, garage, and roof access where applicable.

If the team arrives on-site and access is not provided or is significantly delayed, no refund shall be issued. A re-inspection will be treated as a new booking at the full fee.

Scope of Inspection

The inspection covers all visible and accessible areas of the property. Concealed works, areas behind walls, above false ceilings, below raised floors, or underground elements are excluded unless specialist equipment is deployed by prior agreement.

If the unit is furnished or any areas are obstructed by the developer, contractor, or hirer, SnaggingPro shall not be held responsible for defects in those inaccessible areas.

Liability & Best Effort

SnaggingPro operates on a best-effort basis. While every reasonable measure is taken to identify defects, the hirer acknowledges that SnaggingPro cannot be held liable for any items that may have been missed. The report does not constitute a structural survey or engineering certification.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

The hirer must provide a minimum of 24 hours' written notice to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice incur a fee of 50% of the agreed inspection fee. Same-day cancellations or no-shows are non-refundable.

Re-inspection

The initial inspection is the contracted scope. Any re-inspection conducted after developer or contractor rectification works is a separate service charged at an agreed fee. Re-inspection is recommended to verify all snags have been properly resolved.

Report Validity & Ownership

The report reflects the condition of the property at the time and date of inspection only. SnaggingPro is not responsible for defects that develop, are introduced, or become apparent after the inspection date.

The report is prepared exclusively for the hirer. SnaggingPro holds no liability if the report is shared with or relied upon by third parties.

Photography

By engaging SnaggingPro, the hirer consents to photographs being taken of all areas of the property for documentation and reporting purposes. Photos are used solely for the inspection report and are not shared publicly or with any third party without the hirer's consent.

Governing Law

These terms shall be governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates. Any disputes arising from this engagement shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the Dubai Courts. SnaggingPro shall not be liable for any delays or failures caused by circumstances beyond its reasonable control.
